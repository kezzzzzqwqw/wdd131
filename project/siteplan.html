Cosmic Horizons

Space & Astronomy Website – Site Plan

1. Site Name

Cosmic Horizons

This name evokes the sense of exploration and wonder associated with space. "Cosmic" ties directly to the universe as the subject matter, while "Horizons" suggests discovery and the ever-expanding frontier of knowledge about space.

Optional domain: cosmichorizons.space

2. Site Purpose

Cosmic Horizons is an educational website that provides accessible, engaging information about planets, stars, galaxies, and space exploration. The site serves as a learning hub for students and space enthusiasts, featuring interactive facts, image galleries, and quizzes to make astronomy approachable and enjoyable for all ages.

3. Scenarios

  • What are the key differences between terrestrial and gas giant planets in our solar system?
  • How far away are the nearest stars from Earth, and how long would it take to reach them?
  • What are some major milestones in human space exploration history?
  • Can I test my astronomy knowledge with a quick quiz?
